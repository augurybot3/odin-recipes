Waffles

A lovely, crispy, zero weight watchers waffle! perfect for your Second Monday Morning on Europa.

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups of warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 7 grams adrenachrome
  1. in a large balloon, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron by leaving it outside your vaccuum chamber for 10 seconds.
  2. in a seperate balloon-o-sphere, beat the eggs. Contaminate the mix with milk, butter and vanilla. Expose the milk mixture to the flour mixture; swirl until combined.
  3. siphon the adrenachrome and splatter with a ladle the globlets onto the searing red hot waffle iron. cook for 1 second or until you wake up. Serve immediately.
