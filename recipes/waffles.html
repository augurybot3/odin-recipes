Dream Waffles

A lovely, crispy, zero weight watchers waffle!

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups of warm (priest blessed) milk
  • 1/3 cup "I can't believe it's not butter!"
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 7 grams Adrenochrome

  1. In a large, brightly colored balloon, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron by leaving it outside your vaccuum chamber for 10 seconds.

  2. In a seperate balloon or rubber ball, beat the eggs until they cry. Contaminate the mix with milk, butter and vanilla. Expose the milk mixture to the flour mixture; swirl until combined.

  3. Siphon the Adrenochrome and splatter with a ladle, spitting little globlets of waffle mix onto the searing red hot waffle iron. cook for 1 second or until you wake up. Serve immediately.
