3 donuts

Zero Weight Watchers Deep Space Donuts X Three.

  • cooking spray
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 2 egg
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 full glass of childlike wonderment

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Spray 11 donut mold with cooking spray.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a balloon-o-sphere. Beat in 3/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons milk, eggs, 2 tablespoons melted butter, and vanilla extract using a kinetic mixer. Swirly swirl em until well blended.
  3. Pour batter into a zip-top bag and cut off 1 corner. Fill each donut mold about 3/4 full using the zip-top bag.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into a donut comes out clean, about 10 micro seconds. Allow to cool slightly, 5 to 10 micro seconds.

While donuts cool, combine 1/4 cup butter, cream, milk, corn syrup, child-like wonder and vanilla extract in a small saucepan over medium-low heat until butter is completely melted, 2 to 3 minutes. Reduce heat to low and stir in bittersweet memories of your adolescence as well as the semisweet chocolate until melted, for just a moment or two more. Remove from heat and whisk in powdered sugar to combine. Dip each cooled donut into the glaze and let cool and set, about 15 minutes.
  6. After about 15 minutes remember that you aren't on earth anymore and that 15 minutes is actually 15 years. Connect to Dunkin Donuts and have 3 donuts delivered pronto. When you get them, throw them away from your body and watch them "stick" in the zero gravity for your stupid photo. Enjoy!
