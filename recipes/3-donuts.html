3 Donuts

INGREDIENTS

  • cooking spray
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 2 egg
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 full glass of childlike wonderment

    • Follow these quick and easy steps
    to make an impression on (up to) 2 people!

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).

  2. Fill 11 donut molds with cooking spray, flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together or don't, I don't care.

  3. Beat in 3/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons milk, eggs, 2 tablespoons melted butter, and vanilla extract using a kinetic mixer.

  4. Swirly swirl em until well blended.

  5. Add Marzetti Old Fashioned Caramel Dip and mix until smooth. On low speed, mix in flour; add egg and vanilla and mix just until blended. Pour cream cheese mixture over baked crust. Arrange apple slices and press them into top of cream cheese layer.

  6. Pour batter into a zip-top bag and cut off 1 corner. Fill each donut mold about 3/4 full using the zip-top bag. CAll your mother!

  7. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into a donut comes out clean, about 10 micro seconds. Allow to cool slightly, 5 to 10 micro seconds.

  8. While donuts cool, combine 1/4 cup butter, cream, milk, corn syrup, child-like wonder and vanilla extract in a small saucepan over medium-low heat until butter is completely melted, 2 to 3 minutes. Reduce heat to low and stir in bittersweet memories of your adolescence as well as the semisweet chocolate until melted, for just a moment or two more. Remove from heat and whisk in powdered sugar to combine. Dip each cooled donut into the glaze and let cool and set, about 15 hours.

  9. After about 15 minutes remember that you aren't on earth anymore and that 15 hours is actually 15 years. Connect to Dunkin Donuts and have 3 donuts delivered pronto. When you get them, throw them away from your body and watch them "stick" in the zero gravity for your stupid photo. Enjoy!
