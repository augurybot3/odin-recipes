Zero Weight Cupcakes

Award winning zero weight cupcakes are so light they literally float in the air.

They are available in a variety of flavors and come in fun, haute couture wrappers that will make anyone feel as though they are indulging.

Best of all, they do not melt and leave absolutely no mess.

Zero Weight Cupcakes are a small portion of heaven, with no calories and no fat.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of clear gelatin
  • 1 box of cupcake ready mix
  • 2 lemons / 3 blood oranges
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 gram of pectin-ex
  • 2 ounces of colgate toothpaste
  • 4 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups of warm (priest blessed) milk
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/4 cup of lemon juice
  • 2 cups of heavy whipping cream
  • 1/3 cup "I can't believe it's not butter!"
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 smartphone or desktop computer with internet connection

DIRECTIONS

  1. Follow the instructions on the box of cupcake mix
  2. Put the rest of the ingredients around the kitchen to make it look like you did something
  3. Eat the cupcakes then call a professional because yours don't look like the picture
